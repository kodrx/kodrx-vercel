What is KodRx?

KodRx is a healthtech platform for medical prescription issuance and operational validation. Its purpose is to reduce fraud, improve traceability between physicians and pharmacies, and document the prescription lifecycle from issuance to dispensing.

Medical issuance

Physicians generate digital prescriptions with a unique identifier, QR verification, medications, instructions and professional prescriber data.

Pharmacy validation

Pharmacies can scan the prescription, review the necessary dispensing information and register full or partial dispensing events.

Technology-based traceability

Each prescription can include a hash, block reference and verifiable record to strengthen authenticity, auditability and operational tracking.

How it works

KodRx structures the physician-pharmacy workflow so each prescription can be consulted, validated and traced clearly.

The physician issues the prescription The prescription is generated from a medical panel with clinical data, medications and instructions.
KodRx generates a QR and unique identifier The prescription is linked to a verifiable ID and can be printed or accessed digitally.
The pharmacy scans and validates The pharmacy module allows prescription lookup, medication review and dispensing registration.
The system preserves traceability It records who dispensed the medication, when, which item was dispensed and whether it was partial or complete.

Security, privacy and verification

KodRx separates public information from private medical information. The public view allows authenticity verification without exposing sensitive clinical patient data.

Public verification

Allows users to verify the existence of a prescription, review medications and instructions, and check its technology-based verification status.

Operational access

Authorized users can access role-specific functions for physicians, pharmacies and administrators.

KodRx is designed as a complementary digital traceability and operational platform. It does not replace official regulatory mechanisms or institutional healthcare validation systems.

Built with an interoperability vision

KodRx is being developed with a long-term vision toward healthcare interoperability. Its architecture is oriented to support structured data, prescription traceability, pharmacy validation workflows and future integration with clinical data standards.

Building the future of digital medical prescriptions

KodRx is currently in an advanced functional stage, with medical issuance, public verification, pharmacy validation and dispensing traceability modules.
